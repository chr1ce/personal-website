Game Development

Video games have always been special to me, in the way that they allow you to interact with a new world so intimately. I want to make something even half as impactful as those classics that I treasure.

About me:

I'm a current student at the Georgia Institute of Technology, where my studies focus on the AI and Graphics applications of CS. In addition to this focus, I find learning algorithms and computer organization to be incredibly interesting, so I enjoy spending time in those areas as well.

I like to work in a loosely creative sphere, and so most of my projects until now have been in game development and similar creative pursuits. My philosophy is that there is a careful balance to be struck between the classics and innovation, as well as between freedom and limitation, so I try to find that balance in my work.
