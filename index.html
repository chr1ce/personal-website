About me:

I'm a current student at the Georgia Institute of Technology, where my studies focus on the AI and Graphics applications of CS. In addition to this focus, I find learning algorithms and computer organization to be incredibly interesting, so I enjoy spending time in those areas as well.

I like to work in a loosely creative sphere, and so most of my projects until now have been in game development and similar creative pursuits. My philosophy is that there is a careful balance to be struck between freedom and structure, since a beautiful project is nothing without strong direction and purpose, without a defined form, and so I try to find that balance in my work.

Projects:

Lift Simple

A command-line emulating lift tracking and recommendation tool created in C using the ncurses library.

Byte the Bullet

A fast-paced, educational, and collaborative coding game created for HackGT X.

Frieren Dungeon

A simple game created in C for the mGBA emulator, with custom graphics.

Bocchi the Slug!

A platformer with unique movement mechanics and a custom in-game map creator and editor, starring Bocchi!
