About me:

I'm currently attending the Georgia Institute of Technology, studying Computer Science with a focus on Systems Design and Computer Graphics. Over the past four years studying this field, I've had the privilege of working on a wide range of projects, from hardware simulations and operating systems to video-editing desktop applications. I am primarily interested in the design and integration of systems from ideation through development, and the choices affecting efficiency, scope, and usability throughout that process.
