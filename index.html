About me:

I'm a current student at the Georgia Institute of Technology, where my studies focus on the AI and Graphics applications of CS. In addition to this focus, I find learning algorithms and computer organization to be incredibly interesting, so I enjoy spending time in those areas as well.

I like to work in the creative sphere, since I believe that creating something in one's area of interest is the greatest expression of acquired knowledge. Most of my projects until now have been in game development, although I try to branch out often and learn about other areas like IT and web development.

One of my core beliefs is that there is a careful balance to be struck between freedom and structure, since an ambitious, beautiful idea requires strong direction and purpose, a well defined form, to truly flourish. I try to find that balance in my work through thorough planning and frequently reassessing the path that my development should take alongside those working with me.

Projects:

Lift Simple

A command-line emulating lift tracking and recommendation tool created in C using the ncurses library.

Byte the Bullet

A fast-paced, educational, and collaborative coding game created for HackGT X.

Frieren Dungeon

A simple game created in C for the mGBA emulator, with custom graphics.

Bocchi the Slug!

A platformer with unique movement mechanics and a custom in-game map creator and editor, starring Bocchi!
