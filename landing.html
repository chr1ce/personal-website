About me:

I'm a current student at the Georgia Institute of Technology, where my studies focus on the AI and Graphics applications of CS. In addition to this focus, I find learning algorithms and computer organization to be both interesting and incredibly valuable to my overall understanding of CS.

I like to work in a loosely creative sphere, and so most of my projects until now have been in game development and similar creative pursuits. My philosophy is that there is a careful balance to be struck between the classics and innovation which births new, better things, and so I try to find that balance in my work.
